### Chapter 3: The System Under Pressure

Asset priority:
- Highest next asset priority.

Visual concept:
- A command-room and pressure-diagram hybrid.
- The UN/institutional layer should feel organized and procedural.
- The enforcement-limit layer should feel tense, political, and constrained.

Suggested image direction:
- 16:9 or 3:2 homepage mockup image.
- Dark navy and blue-black background.
- Subtle chamber/control-room architecture.
- Central diagram: rules, institutions, state choices, outcomes.
- Side panels: Security Council, General Assembly, legal process, veto/consent/political will.
- Visual accents: cyan institutional lines, muted gold pressure/warning accents.

Avoid:
- Purely decorative abstract shapes.
- Heavy red danger styling.
- Too much paragraph text inside the image.
- Making the UN look like it controls the whole world.

Mockup text to include:
- `CHAPTER 3`
- `The System Under Pressure`
- `Institutions organize cooperation. Politics tests the limits.`
- `Coordination`
- `Consent`
- `Veto`
- `Enforcement`

### Chapter 4: West Philippine Sea Case File

Asset priority:
- Second next asset priority.

Visual concept:
- Case-study-first dossier.
- Maritime map, timeline, evidence cards, and final thesis strip.

Suggested image direction:
- 16:9 or 3:2 homepage mockup image.
- Deep ocean blue and midnight background.
- Map-like maritime grid with subtle island/sea-zone contours.
- Timeline cards: 2012, 2013, 2016, Post-2016.
- Comparison panel: legal clarity versus political reality.
- Bottom closeout strip with compact references/source trust.

Avoid:
- Overly militarized imagery.
