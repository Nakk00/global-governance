# Post-Award Compliance and Diplomacy Record

## Approved Course Claim

The 2016 South China Sea arbitral award clarified legal rights, but it did not create an automatic enforcement mechanism. The post-award period therefore shows a compliance gap: legal clarity strengthened the Philippines' diplomatic and accountability position while practical change still depended on state behavior, maritime presence, alliances, public communication, and sustained political pressure.

## Course Use

Use this source for suggested prompts about post-award compliance, implementation limits, and the difference between winning a legal ruling and changing behavior at sea. The core teaching point is that institutions and law can clarify claims and shape legitimacy, but enforcement remains politically constrained when powerful states resist or ignore the result.
